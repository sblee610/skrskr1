Skr Skr?

Skr Skr is a global communication agency. We are creators offering solutions in advertisment, spreading clients identity and developing external communication strategies.

Our work encompasses graphics design, products and packaging, exhibitions and installations, websites and digital experiences, filming, advertising and communication.

Our structure is equitable. We are creators serving our clients, involving with you and focusing on exploding your passion, making you be part of the creation process.

"Skr"​ means a common reaction when someone suggests something completely ridiculous that is generally a bad idea. However, we reward crazy ideas, turning them around and translating them into creative solutions.

You can say "Skr Skr"​ when you go crazy florishing passionate ideas.
